Australian Grand Prix Weekend Attendance (1996-2026)
Melbourne has hosted the Australian Grand Prix since 1996. After years of steady figures, the post-pandemic era, supercharged by Oscar Piastri's rise, has driven attendance to record-breaking levels, cementing Albert Park as one of the world's best-attended race weekends.
Record territory: The 2026 Australian Grand Prix attracted 483,934 fans across four days, nearly 160,000 more than the last pre-pandemic race in 2019, and closing in on the all-time record of 520,000 set in Adelaide in 1995.

Global Context

Australia in the World of F1

Albert Park is one of the very few Formula 1 venues in the Southern Hemisphere, making Australia a genuinely unique presence on the global calendar, and one of the sport's most passionate fanbases anywhere in the world.

Formula 1 Circuits Around the World — 2026 Calendar
Of the 24 races on the 2026 Formula 1 calendar, only three are held in the Southern Hemisphere. Albert Park in Melbourne is highlighted in red, a truly unique venue on a calendar heavily weighted towards Europe and the Middle East.
